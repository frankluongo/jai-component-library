Gemological Services Offered

Whether you are looking for a written appraisal document or an estate jewelry consultant, this process can be done while you watch in my office or yours, the privacy of your home or bank and at a conveniently located jewelry partner near you.

Appraisal Types

  • Insurance Appraisal

    To establish the average retail replacement value for your jewelry and provide a basis for obtaining insurance coverage for loss, damage or theft.

  • Estate / Probate

    To establish the Fair Market Value for your jewelry and provide a basis for establishing tax liability and/or the distribution of the jewelry between the heirs.

  • Fair Market Value

    A Fair Market Value appraisal is requested by an owner who wishes to convert their jewelry item(s) into cash.

  • Selling your jewelry

    This process involves more than placing a monetary value on your jewelry. It is a step by step consultation of understanding the value of your jewelry and the appropriate secondary market to sell your jewelry. Jewelry Appraisal Inc, specializes in diamonds, colored stones and designer vintage jewelry.

  • Gemological services

    Jewelry Appraisal Inc, offers services in the complex areas of gemstone identification, synthetic and natural stone separation, enhancements and treatments, as well as diamond grading and colored stone grading, pearls and antique and period jewelry.

  • Large collections

    For large collections or estates, I offer on-site appraisal services in the comfort and safety of your home, office or bank. Evaluations can be performed while you watch.

Your Appraisal

In addition to superior customer service, you will receive an accurate and thorough analysis of each piece of jewelry, which includes:

  • Cleaning of item
  • Stone Security inspection
  • Gem identification
  • Metal assay
  • Detailed descriptions
  • Detailed grading analysis of stone(s)
  • Photographs
  • Item valuation
